Scaling Vision
The lunch guide frames the long-term path in three layers.

Daily discipline, connection counting, small acts of restriction and sharing, and measurable consistency.

Pattern recognition across mood, reactivity, study, and growth to suggest relevant practices and passages.
Layer 3: Global Mishkan
A worldwide practice environment where aggregated spiritual activity makes transformation visible at scale.
